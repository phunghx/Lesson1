Introducing Google Now

The right information at just the right time

From knowing the weather before you start your day, to planning the best route to avoid traffic, or even checking your favorite team’s score while they’re playing, Google Now brings you the information you want, when you need it.

Assistance around the clock

Google Now works in the background so you don’t have to. Your information is automatically organized into simple cards that appear just when they’re needed. Because Google Now is ready whenever you are, you can spend less time digging and more time living.

Relevant suggestions

Google Now delivers the information you care about, without you having to search for it. When you choose the cards most relevant to you, Google Now learns how to help manage your day, letting you focus on what matters.

You're in control

Google Now works the way you want. Sign in and Google Now seamlessly brings you customized, highly relevant information. You control the settings that determine the information Google Now provides.

Get Google Now on iOS

Download the Google app

Download the Google app from the Apple App Store.

Open the Google app

Once downloaded, the Google app will appear on your phone’s home screen. Tap on the app to open.

Sign in and swipe up

Sign in to your Google account in the top left corner, and then swipe up on the card at the bottom of the screen to start using Google Now.

Get Google Now on your computer

Before you start using Google Now on your computer, you’ll need to set it up on iOS or Android first.

1. Download and open Chrome

Download and install Chrome, then double click on the Chrome icon to open Chrome.

2. Sign in to Chrome

Enable Google Now in Chrome by clicking on the Chrome menu and choosing "Sign in to Chrome". You'll want to use the same Google account you set up for Google Now on iOS or Android.
